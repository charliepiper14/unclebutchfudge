Root Cause Analysis

Why ROAS Collapsed

Root Cause 1

Creative Fatigue

Video3, Car Review Shortened, and JAN25 PostID are 6 to 12 month old assets running against audiences that have seen them repeatedly. CPMs are rising as Meta struggles to find new reach, and CVR is falling as the message loses impact. Green Screen Video 2 achieves $13.85 CPP in the same campaigns where Video3 costs $28.86. That gap is entirely creative.

Root Cause 2

Budget Misallocation

$34,137 — 85% of total period spend — is sitting on creatives running below 2x ROAS. Only $766 is on creatives running above 3x. The algorithm is being instructed to put the majority of the budget behind its least efficient assets. This is the single largest mechanical cause of the ROAS collapse and it is entirely fixable today.

Root Cause 3

Empty UGC Pipeline

Fresh UGC has been identified as the primary scaling unlock in client communications for six consecutive weeks. Without new creative the algorithm has no efficient path to scale and recycles fatigued assets because there is nothing better to test. This is the structural bottleneck that holds everything else back.
