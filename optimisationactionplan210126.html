Detailed Action Plan

1. Rebuild Advantage+ US Campaign Structure
Execute Today

This campaign drove 58% of 2025 Meta revenue ($68K of $118K). Must replicate structure with proven post IDs.

  • Campaign Setup: Create new Advantage+ Shopping Campaign in UBF account. Name: "YMP - Advantage+ US - Rebuild"
  • Objective: Sales - Purchase conversion event
  • Budget: Start at $500/day (will scale to $1,500/day over 2 weeks)
  • Conversion Location: Website only (not Website & Shop)
  • Pixel: Ensure UBF pixel is selected with Purchase as primary event
  • Geographic Targeting: United States only
  • Existing Customer Budget Cap: Set to 20% to prioritize new customer acquisition
  • Creative: Use existing post IDs from current YMP - Advantage+ - Post IDs campaign (top 5-8 performers)
  • Attribution: 7-day click, 1-day view
  • Bid Strategy: Maximum volume (not cost cap initially)
2. Build BOF Retargeting Layer
Execute Today

Retargeting ran at 3.99X ROAS in 2025. Essential for capturing warm traffic from other channels.

  • Campaign Type: Manual Sales Campaign (not Advantage+)
  • Budget: CBO at $150/day
  • Ad Set 1 - Cart Abandoners: Website Custom Audience - AddToCart last 7 days, exclude Purchase last 7 days
  • Ad Set 2 - Checkout Abandoners: Website Custom Audience - InitiateCheckout last 14 days, exclude Purchase last 14 days
  • Ad Set 3 - Product Viewers: Website Custom Audience - ViewContent last 14 days, exclude AddToCart last 14 days
  • Ad Set 4 - Past Purchasers: Website Custom Audience - Purchase 30-180 days (repeat purchase angle)
  • Creative Strategy: Urgency messaging, social proof, testimonials. Different creative per ad set.
  • Frequency Cap: 3 impressions per day per ad set
  • Placement: Advantage+ placements with manual review after 3 days
3. Rebuild ASC Catalogue Campaign
This Week

Catalogue campaign drove $15K at 2.66X ROAS. Dynamic product ads pull from feed automatically.

  • Pre-requisite: Verify product catalogue is connected to UBF ad account and synced with Shopify
  • Campaign Type: Advantage+ Shopping Campaign (Catalogue)
  • Budget: $300/day
  • Product Set: All products initially, then segment to best sellers after 7 days data
  • Creative: Dynamic creative with catalogue images, prices, product names
  • Primary Text Options: 3-5 variations highlighting custom boxes, freshness, quality
  • Landing Page: Deep link to product pages (not collection)
4. Rebuild TOF Creative Testing Structure
This Week

Creative testing at 3.32X ROAS. Essential for finding winning ads to feed into scaling campaigns.

  • Campaign Type: Manual Sales Campaign - ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization)
  • Structure: 1 ad set per creative concept, $30-50/day per ad set
  • Total Budget: $200-300/day across all test ad sets
  • Audience: Broad targeting (US, 25-65, no interests) - let Meta optimize
  • Test Framework: Test one variable at a time (hook, format, angle, CTA)
  • Success Criteria: 3+ purchases at 2.5X+ ROAS within $100 spend = winner
  • Graduation Process: Winners move to Advantage+ as post IDs within 48 hours
  • Kill Criteria: $50 spend with 0 purchases OR $100 spend under 2X ROAS = pause
5. Rebuild LIN Best-Sellers Campaign
This Week

Interest-based targeting at 3.09X ROAS. Targets users with affinity for fudge, candy, gifting.

  • Campaign Type: Manual Sales Campaign - ABO
  • Budget: $250/day total, split across ad sets
  • Ad Set 1 - Fudge Interest: Interest: Fudge, Chocolate, Confectionery. $75/day
  • Ad Set 2 - Gifting Interest: Interest: Gift baskets, Gift giving, Holiday shopping. $75/day
  • Ad Set 3 - Bakery/Sweets: Interest: Bakery, Candy, Desserts, Sweet foods. $50/day
  • Ad Set 4 - 5% LAL Purchasers: 5% Lookalike of website purchasers (180 days). $50/day
  • Age Range: 25-65 (exclude under 25 - low AOV)
  • Creative: Product-focused ads highlighting Custom Boxes as hero product
6. Scale Current Top Performers
Execute Today

Current campaigns are efficient. Scale incrementally while maintaining ROAS.

  • YMP - Advantage+ - Post IDs: Increase budget from current to $600/day (+25% phase 1)
  • YMP - TOF Winners - CBO: Increase budget from $250 to $350/day (+40%)
  • Scaling Rule: Max 20-25% budget increase per 3-4 days to protect learning phase
  • Monitor: If ROAS drops below 3X after 48 hours, hold budget and assess
  • YMP - Creative Testing: Keep budget stable, focus on finding new winners
  • YMP - Advantage+ Value: Review after 7 days. If still under 2.5X, pause and reallocate budget
