Key Findings

Strong Momentum

ACOS dropped 11.5 points in 14 days (31.5% to 20%) while sales grew 60%. Week 49 hit 17.8% ACOS, nearly reaching the 15% target during peak season.

Branded Wins

Branded campaigns consistently hit 13-15% ACOS, meeting or exceeding targets. These are underspent relative to their efficiency.

Placement Opportunity

Top of Search delivers 18.1% ACOS with 21.4% conversion rate vs 11-12% for other placements. Increase bid modifiers.

PAT Defensive Excellence

SD PAT Defensive campaigns run at 10-13% ACOS, below target. Expand this strategy across all top ASINs.

SBV is King

Sponsored Brands Video at 25.6% ACOS drives 80%+ New-to-Brand orders. Standard SB at 88% ACOS should be consolidated into SBV.

Wasted Spend

$4,615 spent on non-converting search terms in 30 days. Implement negative keyword strategy to reclaim this budget.

Strategic Recommendations

Immediate Actions (Week 1-2)

  1. 1 Increase Top of Search bid modifiers by 50-100% on top performing campaigns to capture more 18% ACOS traffic
  2. 2 Scale branded campaign budgets by 25-30%. Already at/below 15% target with room to grow
  3. 3 Implement negative keywords from wasted spend analysis (target top 50 non-converting terms)
  4. 4 Increase budgets on 4 budget-capped campaigns (Fudge Custom Box Exact at 23% ACOS)
  5. 5 Re-enable high-performing paused SD PAT Defensive campaigns

Short-Term Actions (Week 3-4)

  1. 6 Shift 20% of Auto campaign spend to Manual Exact match (24.5% vs 38.4% ACOS)
  2. 7 Reduce Phrase match bids by 15-20%, reallocate to Exact match
  3. 8 Pause Off-Amazon placements (zero ROAS)
  4. 9 Consolidate SB Standard campaigns into SBV format
  5. 10 Reduce bids on underperforming ASINs (>40% ACOS)

Expected Impact

Implementing these recommendations should drive ACOS from current 24.8% toward the 15-18% target range within 30-45 days, while maintaining or increasing sales volume. This positions Amazon to contribute efficiently to the 25% blended MCoS goal.
