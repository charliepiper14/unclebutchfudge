Why 15 to 20% weekly

Faster than 25% weekly growth resets Meta's optimisation. The algorithm interprets large jumps as new campaigns and starts learning over. We saw this happen on the original collapse and it cost four weeks to recover.

Why ROAS gates the step

Each step-up only happens when ROAS clears 2.5x for a sustained read. If the audience and creative are not absorbing efficiency at the current spend, more spend buys frequency, not revenue. The gate protects the curve.

Faster path exists

If creative quality holds through the next two rotations and new audiences absorb at L8 efficiency, 25% weekly is safe and shaves 2 to 3 weeks off every threshold. Conservative number first, beat it second.

The offer construction

Discounting trains customers to wait for sales and erodes the brand at the cart. The Hormozi approach is to stack perceived value at full price so the sticker looks like a bargain for what the customer is getting. Goal is revenue lift at protected margin.

Core product

Custom Memorial Day Variety Box at full standard pricing.

Free bonus stack

  • Handwritten Memorial Day card with personalised message option
  • Guaranteed delivery by Memorial Day or full refund plus we overnight a replacement
  • Free 2-piece Thank You mini box for the buyer, so the gift giver tastes alongside
  • Lifetime 10% loyalty code for repeat orders, zero cost until they reorder at margin
  • Entry into a Mom of the Year giveaway, year of free fudge for the winner

Risk reversal

"If your guests don't text you the moment they open this box, we'll refund every cent and you keep the fudge."

Scarcity + urgency

Only 500 boxes available, hand-poured fresh. Order by midnight Friday for guaranteed Memorial Day arrival.

Why this beats 20% off

  • Margin stays at full. Every dollar of revenue carries the same gross margin as standard sales
  • AOV likely lifts. The offer story makes people choose the larger box because the value stack feels disproportionate
  • Premium positioning protected. No discount conditioning. Customers continue buying at full price after the promotion ends
  • Email and SMS list grows. Giveaway entries feed Georgie's flow with high-intent buyers
  • Loyalty pipeline activates. The 10% lifetime code creates repeat purchase momentum that compounds beyond Memorial Day

Test the offer copy against control in the first 24 hours. If signals are weak, swap the headline angle by Day 2. Don't ride a flat offer for 4 days hoping it improves.

Inventory or fulfillment constraint

At $5K to $8K daily, order volume increases substantially. Custom box flavour availability or shipping capacity could constrain the run.

Mitigation

Confirm inventory and fulfillment headroom for May before Week 2 step-up. Pause specific flavour ads if we're tight on stock rather than risk customer experience.
