Creative Guidelines

Hook in First 1-2 Seconds

TikTok users scroll fast. Open with visual intrigue or bold statement.

Native Feel Over Polish

UGC-style content outperforms branded ads. Keep it authentic.

Sound On Experience

Unlike Meta, TikTok users expect audio. Use trending sounds when possible.
