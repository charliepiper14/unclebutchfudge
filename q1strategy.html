Operating Principles

Seven Rules for Q1 Execution

01 Blended Targets Over Platform Perfection

25% MCoS is the goal. Individual channels can flex if the portfolio holds.

02 Methodical Scaling Beats Aggressive Moves

20 to 25% budget increases protect learning phases. No dramatic shifts.

03 Front Load Creative Production

Build the 90 day pipeline now. Creative fatigue is predictable.

04 Landing Pages Before Scaling

Run conversion tests before budget pushes. 16% improvement beats bid tweaks.

05 Branded Search as Profit Engine

Every lost impression share point at 6X+ ROAS is leaving money on the table.

06 Post Promotion Recovery Windows

5 to 7 days of stable budgets after flash sales let algorithms normalize.

07 Data Transparency Builds Trust

Weekly wrap ups with precise metrics. No surprises, just progress.
